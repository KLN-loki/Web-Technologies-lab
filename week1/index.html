Nuts and Legumes

Nuts and legumes contain essential vitamins, minerals and plant chemicals. They also contain fiber. There are numerous types of nuts and legumes available and countless ways to prepare, cook and serve them. A diet rich in nuts and legumes can help shield you against cancer, diabetes and heart disease. Consume 5 varieties of legumes and 2 kinds of nuts each day for robust health. The majority of individuals do not consume adequate nuts and legumes. When purchasing and serving nuts and legumes, strive for variety to obtain the maximum nutrients and allure.
